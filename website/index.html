Great Features

  • Save Bandwidth

    By reducing the image sizez that are loaded onto your webpage, this extension ensures you use lower bandwidth and thus can run your websites at optimal speeds at suboptimal internet service.

  • Fast Loading Times

    Having to load only the processed images at lower sizes, you are guaranteed to load your webpages at similiar if not drastically lower times due to less cache usage.

  • Control of Your Webpage

    With this extension, you have the ability to control how your content is viewed and at what sizes are images produced to you. Take command of your viewing experience and create the best viewing conditions for yourself.
