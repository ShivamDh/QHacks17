Great Features

  • Save Bandwidth

    By reducing the image sizez that are loaded onto your webpage, this extension ensures you use lower bandwidth and thus can run your websites at optimal speeds at suboptimal internet service.

  • Fast Loading Times

    Having to load only the processed images at lower sizes, you are guaranteed to load your webpages at similiar if not drastically lower times due to less cache usage.

  • Control of Your Webpage

    With this extension, you have the ability to control how your content is viewed and at what sizes are images produced to you. Take command of your viewing experience and create the best viewing conditions for yourself.

Our team believes that the best browsing experience comes when one doesn't have to worry about slow load times or internet bandwidth usage. By using Quik Pic, you can be guaranteed to be able to let go of these restrictions and freely browse on the internet. The extension finds and sends all images from the webpage you are visiting, sending it to a backend server, where the image changed to a smaller size while trying to maintain as much quality and resolution as possible. Quik Pic is a revolutionary and innovative solution to one of the most common problem on the web, and you can be assurred that using this extension will enhance your time on the internet.
